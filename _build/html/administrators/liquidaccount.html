Website Visitors¶

Follow yourself on your website IQNOMY will compose visitors profile for every website visitor. How can I look up my own profile? First you need to find your Visitor ID (visitor ID). Chrome:

  1. Go to your website (IQNOMY script has to be implemented)
  2. Click right mouse button ‘Inspect element’
  3. Select tab ‘Network’
  4. Click F5
  5. Search URL starting with http://liquifier.iqnomy.com/myliquidsuite-ws/
  6. Click on the URL – it will appear on the right side. In the URL you can find a VID = 10 numbers
  7. Copy the VID
